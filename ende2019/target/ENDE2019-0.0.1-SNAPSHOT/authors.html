Instructions:

Before submitting your paper, please find the button menu on the right side of the main page, then click the "Register" button to register your ENDE2019 website account. If you've already registered, please log in to your account by clicking the "Login" button on the button menu. All papers should be submitted via the online submission system which can be entered by clicking the "Submit" button on the button menu once you logged in. Papers must be prepared in PDF (Portable Document Format) file. Any formats other than PDF will not be considered for review. Please note that the filename of paper should include the initials of the topic name and the author’s name, e.g.

For a paper in Integrated ENDT&E Systems, the filename is IES_Name.doc

The templates of the two-page paper (abstract) and the full paper can be found in: abstract [Word_*.doc] full paper [Word_instructions_*.rar] [LaTex_instructions_*.rar]

Please submit your 2-page abstract to ENDE2019 before Apr. 30, 2019. If you encounter any problems that prevent you from registration or submission, please use a different browser (recommend Google Chrome, Apple Safari, Microsoft Edge, and Firefox) or clean the cookie of the browser. If the problem still not resolved, please contact us via: ende2019@sina.com.


Guidelines for Poster presentation:


Poster sessions will be held in the Exhibit Hall of the conference venue. Please use a standard poster size such as 48 inches (H) X 36 inches (W) or 1200 mm (H) X 900mm (W).


During poster sessions, no other event will be programmed. Students who are participating in the “Student Poster Competition” are encouraged to present a 3-minute technical talk with Q&A. This talk will be recorded.
