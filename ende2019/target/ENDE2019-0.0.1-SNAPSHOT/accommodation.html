Transportation:

The Longemont Hotels Chengdu is located in the north exit of the Eastern Square of Chengdu East Railway Station - the No.1 comprehensive transportation hub in Southwest China. Hotel guests can access directly to the arrival floor of East Railway Station and Metro Station (Line 2 and Line 7) via the underground tunnel from the hotel B2. It only takes 16 minutes to Chengdu Shuangliu International Airport by Cheng-Mian-Le high speed train. The ENDE committee will offer free airport pick-up services for all attendees during 10th and 11st Sept, please send an advance notice of arriving date and flight number to ENDE2019@sina.com.
