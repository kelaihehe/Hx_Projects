The 24th International Workshop on Electromagnetic Nondestructive Evaluation (ENDE2019)

(Paper submission is now open, all contributions and participation are welcomed)

Longemont Hotels in Chengdu, Sichuan, China

September 11-14, 2019

The 24th International Workshop on Electromagnetic Nondestructive Evaluation (ENDE2019) will be held on September 11-14, 2019, at the Longemont Hotels in Chengdu, Sichuan, China.

This year's technical sessions, student posters competition and other special events will be hosted by University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, Sichuan University and Southwest Jiaotong University. The subjects of the 24th workshop will focus on the theoretical and applied research on Electromagnetic Non-destructive Evaluation methods and provide a wonderful opportunity to exchange ideas and update on the latest developments.

We welcome you to join us for an exciting program in “the Land of Abundance” in 2019!

Call for Paper:

Authors are invited to submit 2-page short papers (full papers are also welcomed) relevant to the workshop topics no later than 30th May. Papers should present original work not submitted or published elsewhere. All papers are to be reviewed by Scientific Committee of ENDE 2019. The accepted ones will be included in the workshop proceeding. Authors of accepted short papers are encouraged to submit full papers (4 to 6 pages), which will be peer reviewed for inclusion in a volume of the ISTP (EI) indexed book series “Studies on Applied Electromagnetic and Mechanics”, published by IOS Press. Outstanding papers will be recommended to SCI indexed journals.
