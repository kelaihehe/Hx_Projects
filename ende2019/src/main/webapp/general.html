About ENDE

The International Electromagnetic Nondestructive Evaluation workshop, a.k.a. ENDE, has been held every year since 1995. Its aim is to bring together engineers and scientists from universities, research institutions, government and industry who are active in research, development and industrial applications of electromagnetic nondestructive evaluation. The 24th ENDE workshop will be held in Chengdu, China and jointly organized by University of Electronic Science and technology of China, Sichuan University and Southwest Jiaotong University.
